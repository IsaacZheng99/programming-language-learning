## 8. Control Flow Statement

**Knowledge Points**:

1. `continue`
2. `break`
3. `return`

### 1. continue

1. Only used in `loop`.
2. Go to the next iteration.

### 2. break

1. Used in `loop` and `switch` statements.
2. Get out of the `loop` or `switch`.

### 3. return

1. Used in anywhere.
2. Get out of the `function`.
