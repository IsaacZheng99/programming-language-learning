## 8. Control Flow Statement

**Knowledge Points**: `continue`, `break`, `return`

1. **continue**:

    1. only used in `loop`
    2. go to the next iteration

2. **break**: 

    1. used in `loop` and `switch` statement
    2. get out of the `loop` or `switch`

3. **return**: 

    1. used in anywhere
    2. get out of the function
